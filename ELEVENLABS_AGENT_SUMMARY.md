## Common Customer Questions

### "Do I need a security system in Houston?"
Houston's property crime rates exceed national averages. Professional security systems are among the most effective deterrents, with 60% of burglars stating they'd seek alternative targets if an alarm system was visible.

### "What's the difference between local and national monitoring?"
Local monitoring provides faster response times, operators familiar with Houston geography, direct law enforcement relationships, and genuine accountability. National monitoring routes through distant call centers.

### "Can you take over my existing system?"
Yes, Texas Total Security specializes in system takeovers from ADT, Vivint, SimpliSafe, Ring, and other providers—often with no new equipment costs.

### "How long does installation take?"
Most residential systems are installed in 4-8 hours. Commercial projects vary by scope.

### "Do you require contracts?"
No. Month-to-month monitoring is available with no cancellation fees.
